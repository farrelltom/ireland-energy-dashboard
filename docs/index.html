More context

  • Wind was 23% of generation yesterday, down 18 percentage points vs last week.
  • Grid CO₂ intensity averaged 243 gCO₂/kWh yesterday.
  • System demand averaged 4303 MW yesterday (wind supplied roughly 992 MW of that).
  • Ireland was a net importer of electricity yesterday, drawing an average of 904 MW from interconnectors.
  • EV (home charging at €0.40/kWh) costs €7.20/100km vs €12.41/100km for petrol — 42% cheaper.
